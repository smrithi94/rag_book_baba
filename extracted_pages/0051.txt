Baba in Havana, Cuba
Baba sent a message at lights-out time, 9 p.m., that we should be 
ready at 5 a.m. to ride with him on his return to Bangalore. The 
departure was confidential and not a word was to be said to 
anyone. We were elated, as may be imagined. This was our first 
invitation.
At 4:30 in the morning we were ready, and with our small amount of 
luggage walked towards the driveway. Our first surprise was to find a 
line of cars in the lane leading to the outside road, obviously 
prepared to get behind Baba's car at the very moment of his 
departure. But how could this be? Nobody was supposed to know. 
Puzzled, we continued to the Mandir (i.e. the temple) where we were 
to meet Baba. Another surprise! The entire area in front of the 
Mandir was filled with people sitting on the ground, in silence â€” no 
conversation, not even whispers. This was to be a secret departure?
